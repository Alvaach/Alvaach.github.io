Murmurs of the Mist

  • Gameplay

    Traverse through a small child’s inner thoughts and memories, with her plushy Bunni as your guide. Explore, uncover and solve puzzles to understand what has happened, and how a childlike mind twists dark experiences into dreamlike horror stories.

  • Level Design

    Working with level design taught me how details and the symbiosis between lights, props, and sound are essential to maintaining immersion and delivering an eerie atmosphere. I took particular pride in balancing critical path guidance with creative world-building, and seeing YouTubers genuinely explore the hidden lore and details my team crafted made every hour of work feel worthwhile.

  • UI and UX

    Collaborating with the art department, I designed UI elements that balanced immersion with clarity, primarily through instructional text that guided players through controls and puzzles. The most challenging and rewarding piece was the Hangman puzzle, where carefully synchronizing code execution, sound, and animation was key to evoking childhood nostalgia with an unsettling twist.
